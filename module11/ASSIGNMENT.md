## Submission Checklist
Before submitting, make sure your report includes:
- all 7 required section headings
- concise written responses
- annotated screenshots or logs
- evidence of both allowed and denied behavior
- explanation of what changed and why it matters
- two residual risks with suggested improvements

> Important Note: This assignment is not graded as a checklist of interface clicks. The scaffold provided is intended to help you stand up a working environment quickly so that you can focus on security reasoning.

A successful submission must show that you understand:
- what was exposed,
- what control was added,
- where the trust boundary sits,
- what evidence shows the control is functioning,
- and what risks remain.

In other words, do not merely show that the lab works. Show why the design matters.
